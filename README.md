# -iPhone-Sales-Dashboard-Analysis
This dashboard provides a complete overview of iPhone sales performance for the selected month (January). It highlights key sales metrics, product performance, customer preferences, and regional sales distribution using interactive charts and KPIs.
🔹 Key Insights
Total Sales Revenue: 46K
Total Quantity Sold: 112 units
Minimum Product Price: 720
Average Product Price: 1.44K

These KPIs give a quick summary of overall business performance and pricing trends.


📱 Product Performance

The Storage by Count chart shows the sales count of different iPhone models:

iPhone 15 Pro Max has the highest sales count.
Followed by iPhone 14 Pro and iPhone 13.
Lower sales are observed for older models like iPhone 14.

This indicates that customers prefer premium and latest-generation iPhones.

🌍 Country-wise Sales Analysis

The Country by Quantity and Price by Country charts reveal:

UK generated the highest sales quantity and revenue.
Canada and USA also contributed significantly.
Germany, Pakistan, and UAE recorded comparatively lower sales.

This helps identify top-performing markets and future growth opportunities.


💳 Payment Method Analysis

The Price by Payment Method pie chart shows customer payment preferences:

Credit Card payments contribute the highest share (33.11%).
Cash transactions account for 28.21%.
Debit Card usage is also strong.
PayPal has the lowest contribution.

This insight helps businesses understand customer transaction behavior.


🎨 Color Preference Analysis

The Price by Color chart highlights customer color preferences:

White is the most preferred iPhone color (35.97%).
Blue ranks second (28.45%).
Other colors like Black, Purple, and Red have lower sales percentages.

This information can support inventory and marketing decisions.
